Welcome to El-Flourish Farm

El-flourish Farms is a privately owned indigenous farm company located in Choba within the visinity of university of Port harcourt, Rivers State Nigeria. The farm was established in 2022 with the mission to provide organic farm products for its immidiate and neighbouring communities, create employment for the youths in the community and as well as provide training to help build their capacity in agriculture.

BATTERY CAGE SYSTEM

El-Flourish Farms focus on efficiency in the production of poultry products. We use the Battery Cage Systems for our egg-laying birds. This system which is perceived to be the best approach in housing of egg-laying birds.

FEED MILL

At El-Flourish Farm, we are cost efficient in everything we do; in order to reduce the end cost for our clients and also ensure the total safety and quality of feeds.
