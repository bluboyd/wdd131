About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce healthy and affordable poultry products by using latest innovations and through knowledge transfer.

Our Vision

El-Flourish Farms Nigeria Ltd visions is to make chicken and eggs the most available and affordable protein in every home in Rivers State, Nigeria.

Our History

Founded in 2015, our poultry farm has grown from a small family operation to a reputable provider high-quality poultry products and skills for the community. Our core values of integrity, innovations, training and hard work has been the reason behind our growth .
